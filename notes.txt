Attendance Routes on web.php

GET "/frontdesk/attendance/get" (Get all attendance records)
GET "/frontdesk/attendance/find/" (Get 1 attendance record by given id) Parameters: {id}
get '/frontdesk/attendance/dashboard' (Get dashboard completed counts for year, month and day)
get '/frontdesk/attendance/registered'(Get all registered (Waiting) list)
get '/frontdesk/attendance/attending' (Get all currently attending list)
